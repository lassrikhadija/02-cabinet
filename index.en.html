Frequently asked

Wondering about it — you're not alone.

Will it hurt during treatment?
The vast majority of our treatments are pain-free thanks to modern local anesthesia (Wand®, no traditional needle). For anxious patients, we also offer conscious sedation. At any time, you can ask us to stop or take a break.
How much does it cost? Do you accept insurance?
Our indicative prices are openly published (see Pricing section). Before any treatment, we provide a detailed quote. We bill directly to your insurance (27 Canadian insurers: Sun Life, Manulife, Desjardins, Blue Cross, etc.). For children ≤ 10 years, essential care is fully covered by RAMQ.
It's my first visit — what happens?
Plan for 60 minutes. On the agenda: discussion of your history and concerns, complete examination, X-rays if necessary, personalized treatment plan with quote. No treatment is started that day (except emergencies) — you leave with all the information to decide at your pace.
Can my children come? From what age?
Yes — first visit recommended around 2-3 years, as soon as the first teeth appear. Our approach is playful: tour of the office, counting teeth, brushing as a game. Our hygienists are trained for children. Essential care covered by RAMQ up to age 10.
Do you handle emergencies? Evenings, weekends?
Yes — 24/7 emergency line at 514-555-0102. Evenings and weekends, an on-call dentist is reachable and can see you the same day if needed. For acute pain or trauma, we always keep slots open in the week.
Do you speak English and Arabic?
Yes — the team is fluent in French and English. Dr. Nouri also speaks Arabic, and Sofia speaks Spanish. We adapt to your language of comfort to clearly explain every treatment.
